How these projections are built

The Jan 2025 expansion to 40 minutes is our empirical anchor — the only observed data point where catchment length visibly spiked signal contacts. We use that event to model three scenarios for the 40→65 min move.

4.5×
Observed multiplier from the Jan 2025 expansion. Applied to today's baseline, this is the upper-bound "same shock" scenario.
+62.5%
Proportional scaling: catchment window grows from 40→65 min (+62.5%). Signal contacts scale linearly with time spent on-route.
+20%
Conservative floor: drivers and ops have adapted since 2025. Signal issues don't scale 1:1 with time. Diminishing returns apply.
